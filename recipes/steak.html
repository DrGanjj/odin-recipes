Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak of Choice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Place butter in pan and melt. Get it to ~500F.
  2. Add steak, and immedietely salt and pepper. Start timer at the same time.
  3. After 60 seconds, flip steak.
  4. Salt and pepper other side now. Add 2-3x as much salt and pepper on this side as the other, almost creating a crust.
  5. After 60 seconds, flip again. From here on, cook per side based on preference. For medium on a 1" thick steak, assume 3 minutes additional per side.
